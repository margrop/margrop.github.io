Why use Github Page?

Published: 2021-01-13 · 阅读量 --

That was more than ten years ago. I used 163Blog more than ten years ago. Because the blog reposted several articles about building airports and airplanes, 163Blog was permanently banned.

All the articles on 163Blog were instantly wiped out, and I was also hit hard. It is true that once bitten by a snake, I will be afraid of well ropes for ten years.

Later, I used 有道云笔记 to accumulate technology, but 有道云笔记 was too closed. If history repeats itself, the trouble will be quite big.

So now we are slowly reducing our dependence on 有道云笔记. Personal privacy documents are gradually migrated to Synology Notes, while documents that can be made public are gradually migrated to Github.

Recently, the Internet has become more and more regressive. For more than ten years, large files have been downloaded through BT. A few years ago, download tools were almost eliminated. As a result, after the major manufacturers gained a monopoly, they began to charge various membership fees and cloud space fees. The feeling of being strangled is really uncomfortable. If you have the data in your own hands, you won’t be afraid of being bullied by big manufacturers.
